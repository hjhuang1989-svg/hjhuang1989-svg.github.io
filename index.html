About

I am a researcher working on next-generation wireless networks, with interests spanning RIS/STAR-RIS-assisted communications, integrated sensing and communication, secure and energy-aware transmission, UAV-assisted networking, and underwater acoustic channel modeling.

我是一名面向下一代无线网络的科研工作者，研究兴趣涵盖 RIS/STAR-RIS 辅助通信、通感一体化、安全与能量感知传输、无人机辅助组网以及水声信道建模。

Research

  • Intelligent wireless communications and RIS/STAR-RIS. Beamforming, resource allocation, and system optimization for RIS/STAR-RIS-assisted wireless networks.
    智能无线通信与 RIS/STAR-RIS。 面向 RIS/STAR-RIS 辅助无线网络的波束成形、资源分配与系统优化。
  • Integrated sensing, communication, security, and power transfer. Joint design for ISAC, secure communication, and wireless power transfer in future 6G systems.
    通感安全与能量传输一体化。 面向未来 6G 系统的通感一体化、安全通信与无线能量传输联合设计。
  • AI and optimization for UAV/networked systems. Learning-based control, trajectory planning, and optimization for UAV-assisted and dynamic communication environments.
    面向无人机与网络系统的 AI 优化。 面向无人机辅助通信和动态网络环境的学习控制、轨迹规划与优化方法。
  • Underwater acoustic channel modeling and learning-based planning. Channel-aware modeling, simulation, and decision-making for underwater/acoustic communication environments, including learning-based planning under complex propagation conditions.
    水声信道建模与学习型规划。 面向水下/水声通信环境的信道感知建模、仿真与决策方法，关注复杂传播条件下的学习型规划与优化。
